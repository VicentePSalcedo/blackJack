## A Black Jack Break Down

- [ ] Tables usually hold 2-7 players max.

- [ ] As betting rules dictate, players must add their wages to the table 
before the game starts.

- [ ] Starting from their left, the dealr gives one upcard to each player and 
themselves.

- [ ] The dealer deals a second upcard to everyone, finishing with one facedown
for themselves.

- [ ] When it's your tunr, look at your card values and firure out your next 
move.

- [ ] Once you're happy with your hand, stand to end your turn.

- After everyplayer has finished their turn, watch the dealer play out their 
hand.
